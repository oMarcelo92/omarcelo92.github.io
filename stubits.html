Stubits

PC • First Person • 2018

Introduction

StuBits (previously known as Project Trashman) is a first person experience (FPX) game being developed by Sawce, where you control Stu, a type of robot that has to keep and clean an entire factory - while, of course, having fun and throwing trashes at others robots. The game is composed of numerous gamemodes and maps, where each one has its specific set of rules, objectives and obstacles the player must surpass to win.

The main differential of this game is the humour. Most of the game mechanics are made to make the player laugh with his friends. The way damage works (by physics), the robot looks (with dynamic emojis!) and sounds, the puzzles, the level design, everything is made to transmit a comedic feeling to the players, even though the game itself has a competitive side as well, like in race or deathmatch gamemode, where skill and tactics are needed to win.
